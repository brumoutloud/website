About Brum Out Loud

Alright babbies! Brum Out Loud is your bostin' guide to the West Midlands' queer scene. From the legendary Gay Village to hidden gems across the region, we're here to celebrate our bold, brash, and beautiful LGBTQ+ community.

This is a passion project, built by the community, for the community. We believe in making our scene accessible, visible, and easy for everyone to explore.
